I am a machine learning engineer with a passion of making (and playing) games. A full catalogue of previous works I've done or been part of can be found on my [github](https://github.com/dan-ellett)!

Personal profile
======
I graduated from Bournemouth University in Games Software Engineering, specialising in visual programming and agmes technology with strong experience in C++, C# and Python, alongside practical experience in Unreal Engine and Unity, but also right down to the barebones in SDL2.`

I'm a skilled problem solver and communicator, which I then took and used in teaching Computer Science at UK secondary school level. I love to keep learning and thrive in fast paced environments. 


About me
------
I grew up in Bristol, UK but my family come from Poland and Germany, introduced to games at a young age with my dad's Xbox original and PS2. This was only fuelled further by my parent's friends bringing me back damaged games from a disposal factory and helping me clean them up so they'd work. 

In 2022, I made the move to Bournemouth, UK for university where I discovered I am passionate about making games, so I gained experience from multiple collaborative projects in competitive game jams - with our [own small studio](https://github.com/Pierogi-People) to boot. I enjoy getting unreasonably annoyed at games, with a penchant for multiplayer games. I also love filling in colours on map in Paradox games.
